Social VR Force Fields

Due to the immersive nature of Virtual Reality, online harassment in social VR apps like Rec Room can feel quite intrusive. Consequently, people who have bad experiences with other users on these apps can be discouraged from using VR altogether.

As an intern in Google Daydream Labs, my goal was to conceptualize and implement several types of "force fields" that prevented other users from invading your personal space, reducing possibilities for harassment as a result.
